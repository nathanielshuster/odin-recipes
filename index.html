Tuscan Chicken

A Tasty Plate of Tuscan Chicken

  • Chicken
  • Stock
  • Cream
  • Sun Dried Tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Parmesean
  1. Add chicken, stock, and tomatoes to crockpot - cook 4 hrs
  2. Remove chicken and whisk in parmesean and cream
  3. Add chicken back to crockpot - cook 30 min
  4. Add spinach to crockpot - cook until wilted
