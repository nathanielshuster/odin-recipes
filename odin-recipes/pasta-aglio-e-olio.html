Pasta Aglio e Olio

Delicious Pasta Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Dried Hot Peppers
  • Oil
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano

Steps

  1. Cook pasta al dente
  2. In a sauce pan, add garlic, peppers, and oil - cook until garlic is brown
  3. Add pasta to sauce pan and stir
  4. Grate parmiggiano over top
  5. Serve and enjoy
